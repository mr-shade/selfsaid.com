Welcome to this collection of George Harrison’s 50 Best Quotes. [George Harrison](https://www.georgeharrison.com/), known as the quiet Beatle, left behind a legacy filled with wisdom, humor, and a deep sense of spirituality. His words continue to inspire and resonate with fans all around the world.

This article compiles 50 of his most memorable quotes, covering topics from love and life to music and spirituality. Whether you’re a lifelong fan of The Beatles or just someone seeking some inspiration, these [quotes by The Beatles](/beatles-quotes)‘ lead guitarist will surely strike a chord. So, if you’re ready to see the world through George Harrison’s eyes, read on and discover the timeless wisdom he shared through his words.

## George Harrison Quotes

1.  “It’s all in the mind.” – George Harrison
2.  “The Beatles saved the world from boredom.” – George Harrison
3.  “Gossip is the Devil’s radio.” – George Harrison
4.  “Life goes on within you and without you.” – George Harrison
5.  “With our love, we could save the world.” – George Harrison
6.  “The world is ready for a mystic revolution.” – George Harrison
7.  “Happiness is the key to everything.” – George Harrison
8.  “As long as you hate, there will be people to hate.” – George Harrison
9.  “Love one another.” – George Harrison
10.  “Try to realize it’s all within yourself, no one else can make you change.” – George Harrison
11.  “Everybody should have themselves regularly overwhelmed by Nature.” – George Harrison
12.  “Sometimes I feel like I’m actually on the wrong planet.” – George Harrison
13.  “The nicest thing is to open the newspapers and not to find yourself in them.” – George Harrison
14.  “Music is everybody’s possession. It’s only publishers who think that people own it.” – George Harrison
15.  “When you’ve seen beyond yourself, then you may find, peace of mind is waiting there.” – George Harrison
16.  “If you don’t know where you’re going, any road will take you there.” – George Harrison
17.  “The inner light is the true self.” – George Harrison
18.  “I wanted to be successful, not famous.” – George Harrison
19.  “I’m not really a career person; I’m a gardener, basically.” – George Harrison
20.  “The Beatles will go on and on.” – George Harrison
21.  “I play a little guitar, write a few tunes, make a few movies, but none of that’s really me. The real me is something else.” – George Harrison
22.  “We’re all God. I’m not a God or a messiah. But if we’re all God, I’m a part of that.” – George Harrison
23.  “I believe in the religion of love, whatever direction its caravans may take.” – George Harrison
24.  “It’s being here now that’s important. There’s no past and there’s no future. Time is a very misleading thing.” – George Harrison
25.  “I think the biggest problem with modern society is the lack of spirituality.” – George Harrison
26.  “All the world is birthday cake, so take a piece, but not too much.” – George Harrison
27.  “I don’t want to be considered a ‘personality,’ because what I’m doing is not really based on my personality.” – George Harrison
28.  “To achieve spiritual knowledge takes time, like learning a musical instrument. It’s daily practice.” – George Harrison
29.  “You’ve got as many lives as you like, and more, even ones you don’t want.” – George Harrison
30.  “The purpose of life is to find out who am I, why am I here, and where am I going.” – George Harrison
31.  “My idea of heaven is a place where the Tyne meets the Delta, where folk music meets the blues.” – George Harrison
32.  “The main thing is to be honest with yourself, know and recognize your limits and be a sincere person.” – George Harrison
33.  “The only thing we really have to work at in this life is how to manifest love.” – George Harrison
34.  “The farther one travels, the less one knows.” – George Harrison
35.  “We were the Spice Boys.” – George Harrison, joking about The Beatles’ early image
36.  “I’m a Pisces, and they say that Pisces are very [sensitive people](/life-changing-tips-for-sensitive-people). If men were only sensitive, they would go somewhere and meditate and find out the truth.” – George Harrison
37.  “I get confused when I look around at the world, and I see everybody’s running around, and to me, it seems like they’re running in the wrong direction.” – George Harrison
38.  “There’s nothing wrong with making mistakes, just don’t make the same ones.” – George Harrison
39.  “The best thing anyone can do is to try to live in a way that contributes to self-improvement and self-understanding.” – George Harrison
40.  “Your own space, man, it’s so important. That’s why I like the countryside.” – George Harrison
41.  “Materialism and all its miseries can never be conquered by materialism.” – George Harrison
42.  “The more you know, the less you need.” – George Harrison
43.  “I think people who can truly live a life in music are telling the world, ‘You can have my love, you can have my smiles. Forget the bad parts, you don’t need them. Just take the music, the goodness, because it’s the very best, and it’s the part I give most willingly.'” – George Harrison
44.  “Simplicity is something that is beyond the grasp of most people.” – George Harrison
45.  “We need to learn to love ourselves first, in all our glory and our imperfections.” – George Harrison
46.  “The minute you’re born, you’re getting older.” – George Harrison
47.  “I think we all owe a lot more than we can ever pay back.” – George Harrison
48.  “If everyone who had a gun just shot themselves, there wouldn’t be a problem.” – George Harrison
49.  “Death is just where your suit falls off and now you’re in your other suit. You can’t see it on this level, but it’s all right. Don’t worry.” – George Harrison
50.  “Understanding yourself is the key to understanding everything else.” – George Harrison
