Sunny days have a way of lifting spirits and adding a touch of warmth to life. Quotes about sunshine capture those moments of brightness, offering words that bring a smile or a reminder of simpler joys. Here are 50 sunny day quotes to bring a little extra warmth to your day and inspire you to enjoy each moment in the sun.

## Sunny Day Quotes

1.  “Keep your face to the sun and you will never see the shadows.” — Helen Keller
2.  “A sunny disposition is worth more than a fortune.” — Andrew Carnegie
3.  “Wherever you go, no matter what the weather, always bring your own sunshine.” — Anthony J. D’Angelo
4.  “Some old-fashioned things like fresh air and sunshine are hard to beat.” — Laura Ingalls Wilder
5.  “To love and be loved is to feel the sun from both sides.” — David Viscott
6.  “If you spend your whole life waiting for the storm, you’ll never enjoy the sunshine.” — Morris West
7.  “Rise above the storm, and you will find the sunshine.” — Mario Fernandez
8.  “Live in the sunshine, swim in the sea, drink the wild air.” — Ralph Waldo Emerson
9.  “The sun shines not on us but in us.” — John Muir
10.  “Even on a cloudy day, the sun is shining somewhere!” — Unknown
11.  “Sunshine is the best medicine.” — Unknown
12.  “Sunshine is a welcome thing. It brings a lot of brightness.” — Jimmie Davis
13.  “A day without sunshine is like, you know, night.” — Steve Martin
14.  “Friends are the sunshine of life.” — John Hay
15.  “Those who bring sunshine into the lives of others cannot keep it from themselves.” — J.M. Barrie
16.  “It’s the artist’s business to create sunshine when the sun fails.” — Romain Rolland
17.  “Even for me life had its gleams of sunshine.” — Charlotte Brontë
18.  “Just living is not enough… one must have sunshine, freedom, and a little flower.” — Hans Christian Andersen
19.  “Laughter is a sunbeam of the soul.” — Thomas Mann
20.  “Sunshine all the time makes a desert.” — Arabic Proverb
21.  “To be happy, you must be your own sunshine.” — C.E. Jerningham
22.  “Where there is sunshine, the doctor starves.” — Flemish Proverb
23.  “Every morning is a fresh start, a new blessing, a new hope.” — Lailah Gifty Akita
24.  “There is sunshine in my soul today.” — Eliza Hewitt
25.  “When the sun is shining, I can do anything; no mountain is too high, no trouble too difficult to overcome.” — Wilma Rudolph
26.  “Let the sunshine on your heart and warm your soul.” — Unknown
27.  “We must go through the storm to appreciate the sunshine.” — Unknown
28.  “The sun himself is weak when he first rises, and gathers strength and courage as the day gets on.” — Charles Dickens
29.  “If you want to see the sunshine, you have to weather the storm.” — Frank Lane
30.  “The sun will rise and set regardless. What we choose to do with the light while it’s here is up to us.” — Alexandra Elle
31.  “Sunshine is a gift from heaven.” — Unknown
32.  “Faith is the virtue of the storm, love is the virtue of the sunshine.” — Ruth Ross
33.  “I cannot endure to waste anything as precious as autumn sunshine by staying in the house.” — Nathaniel Hawthorne
34.  “It is eternity now. I am in the midst of it. It is about me in the sunshine.” — Richard Jefferies
35.  “To see the summer sky is poetry, though never in a book it lie.” — Emily Dickinson
36.  “O, Sunshine! The most precious gold to be found on Earth.” — Roman Payne
37.  “Light is to darkness what love is to fear; in the presence of one, the other disappears.” — Marianne Williamson
38.  “Bring sunshine into the place you enter.” — Unknown
39.  “A light wind swept over the corn, and all nature laughed in the sunshine.” — Anne Bronte
40.  “Joy is the sunshine of the soul.” — Lailah Gifty Akita
41.  “There is nothing more beautiful than a rainbow, but it takes both rain and sunshine to make a rainbow.” — Roy T. Bennett
42.  “The sun himself is nature’s best fertilizer.” — Unknown
43.  “Smiles are the language of love and sunshine.” — Unknown
44.  “Sunshine, warm and bright, fills the day with golden light.” — Unknown
45.  “The golden hour is an opportunity to bathe in the warmth of life.” — Victoria Erickson
46.  “A good laugh is sunshine in the house.” — William Makepeace Thackeray
47.  “The sun will rise again. It’s a new day, every day.” — Unknown
48.  “The sun watches what I do, but the moon knows all my secrets.” — J.M. Wonderland
49.  “Let your light shine so brightly that others can see their way out of the dark.” — Katrina Mayer
50.  “Sunshine is a reminder that we are loved and life is precious.” — Unknown
