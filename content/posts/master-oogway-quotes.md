Master Oogway, the wise and calm tortoise from Kung Fu Panda, offers many memorable quotes that resonate with fans of all ages. This page gathers some of his best quotes, capturing the wisdom that has made him such an iconic character in the series.

## Master Oogway Quotes

1.  “Remember the path.” Master Oogway
2.  “The past cannot be changed, but the future is still in our power.” – Master Oogway
3.  “The true power of a warrior is not in his strength, but in his ability to control his strength.” – Master Oogway
4.  “[Nothing is impossible](https://selfsaid.30tools.com/nothing-is-impossible-quotes).” – Master Oogway
5.  “The true measure of a man is not how he behaves in moments of comfort and convenience, but how he stands at times of challenge and controversy.” – Master Oogway
6.  “There are no accidents.” – Master Oogway
7.  “There are no limits, only plateaus. And you must not stay there. You must go beyond them.” – Master Oogway
8.  “It is not important to be better than someone else, but to be better than you were yesterday.” – Master Oogway
9.  “The true warrior fights not because he hates to, but because he chooses to.” – Master Oogway
10.  “I sense the Dragon Warrior is among us.” – Master Oogway
11.  “The true test of a warrior is not how much he can handle before he breaks, but how much he can endure after he’s been broken.” – Master Oogway
12.  “Legend tells of a legendary warrior whose kung fu skills were the stuff of legend.” – Master Oogway
13.  “I understand. You eat when you are upset.” – Master Oogway
14.  “The key to success is not just hard work, but also the ability to adapt to change.” – Master Oogway
15.  “You just need to believe. You must believe.” – Master Oogway
16.  “The greatest enemy of knowledge is not ignorance, but the illusion of knowledge.” – Master Oogway
17.  “There is just news. There is no good or bad.” – Master Oogway
18.  “The true measure of a person’s worth is not in what they have, but in what they give.” – Master Oogway
19.  “It is you who must decide whether to stay or go.” – Master Oogway
20.  “The true purpose of life is not to seek happiness, but to find meaning and purpose.” – Master Oogway
21.  “That is bad news… if you do not believe in yourself.” – Master Oogway
22.  “There is always something more to learn. Even for a master.” – Master Oogway
23.  “Before the battle of the Fist, comes the battle of the mind.” – Master Oogway
24.  “The true measure of a master is not in his ability to win but in his ability to lose with dignity.” – Master Oogway
25.  “The first step towards wisdom is humility.” – Master Oogway
26.  “The true path to inner peace is not the absence of conflict, but the ability to cope with it.” – Master Oogway
27.  “You cannot change what has already happened, but you can change how you think about it.” – Master Oogway
28.  “When will you realize? The more you take, the less you have.” – Master Oogway
29.  “The journey of self-discovery is not a matter of finding yourself, but of creating yourself.” – Master Oogway
30.  “The only way to achieve true success is to express yourself completely in service to others.” – Master Oogway
31.  “One often meets his destiny on the road he takes to avoid it.” – Master Oogway
32.  “My time has come. You must continue your journey, without me.” – Master Oogway
33.  “It matters not what someone is born, but what they grow to be.” – Master Oogway
34.  “Good things come to those who wait, greater things come to those who get out there and do anything to make it happen.” – Master Oogway
35.  “I see you have found the Sacred Peach Tree of Heavenly Wisdom!” – Master Oogway
36.  “The true master has no pride; he is always ready to learn.” – Master Oogway
37.  “I think they will all lose until they find a battle worth fighting.” – Master Oogway
38.  “If you only do what you can do, you will never be more than you are now.” – Master Oogway
39.  “Who knows the ways of the universe? Accident? Or destiny? That is the secret.” – Master Oogway
40.  “When the path you walk always leads back to yourself, you never get anywhere.” – Master Oogway
41.  “Maybe it can – if you are willing to guide it, to nurture it, to believe in it.” – Master Oogway
42.  “The only real failure in life is the failure to try.” – Master Oogway
43.  “Look at this tree. I cannot make it blossom when it suits me, nor make it bear fruit before its time.” – Master Oogway
44.  “Use your skills for good, young warrior. Find the one thing you were denied so long ago — compassion.” – Master Oogway
45.  “Yesterday is history, Tomorrow is a mystery, but today is a gift. That is why it is called the present.” – Master Oogway
46.  “The secret to true happiness is to find the balance between who you are and who you want to be.” – Master Oogway
47.  “My time has come. You must continue your journey… without me. You must… believe. Promise me you’ll believe.” – Master Oogway
48.  “Look at this tree, Shifu. I cannot make it blossom when it suits me, nor make it bear fruit before its time.” – Master Oogway
49.  “Shifu, an acorn can only become the mighty oak, not a cherry tree. You must let her grow into what she will be.” – Master Oogway
50.  “My old friend, the panda will never fulfill his destiny, nor you yours, until you [let go of](https://selfsaid.30tools.com/things-to-let-go-of-before-your-next-birthday) the illusion of control.” – Master Oogway
51.  “No matter what you do, that seed will grow to be a peach tree. You may wish for an apple or an orange, but you will get a peach.” – Master Oogway
52.  “Your mind is like this water, my friend. When it is agitated, it becomes difficult to see. But if you allow it to settle, the answer becomes clear.” – Master Oogway
53.  “You are the master of your destiny: No one and nothing can come in between you and your destiny except you. Take destiny by the horns and have fun.” – Master Oogway
54.  “Monkey, you have shown me great skills but I also sense in you, great pain.” – Master Oogway
55.  “Well, good night. We’ve got a big day tomorrow!” – Master Oogway
56.  “Now know this, old friend. Whomever I choose will not only bring peace to the Valley, but also to you.” – Master Oogway
57.  “It doesn’t. It helps me. And by helping me, you help yourself.” – Master Oogway
58.  “No, you just need to believe. Promise me, Shifu. Promise me you will believe.” – Master Oogway
