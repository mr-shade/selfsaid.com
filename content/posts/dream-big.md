## The Meaning Of ‘Dream Big’

The quote “Dream Big” encourages us to set high goals and imagine all the possibilities beyond current limitations. It’s about allowing yourself the freedom to envision a life that reaches beyond immediate surroundings or routine achievements. The idea that aiming high can open up doors to new opportunities and can push you toward making bold, impactful choices.
