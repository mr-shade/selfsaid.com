Elvis Presley, the King of Rock ‘n’ Roll, left behind a legacy filled with incredible music and some memorable quotes that still resonate today. His words offer a glimpse into the mind of a music legend. and the era he helped define. Below you’ll find 50 of his best quotes.

1.  “Truth is like the sun. You can shut it out for a time, but it ain’t goin’ away.” — Elvis Presley
2.  “Ambition is a dream with a V8 engine.” — Elvis Presley
3.  “When things go wrong, don’t go with them.” — Elvis Presley
4.  “Values are like fingerprints. Nobody’s are the same, but you leave ’em all over everything you do.” — Elvis Presley
5.  “It’s not how much you have that makes people look up to you. It’s who you are.” — Elvis Presley
6.  “Do something worth remembering.” — Elvis Presley
7.  “Rhythm is something you either have or don’t have, but when you have it, you have it all over.” — Elvis Presley
8.  “The image is one thing and the human being is another. It’s very hard to live up to an image.” — Elvis Presley
9.  “It’s human nature to gripe, but I’m going ahead and doing the best I can.” — Elvis Presley
10.  “I never expected to be anybody important.” — Elvis Presley
11.  “I’m not trying to be sexy. It’s just my way of expressing myself when I move around.” — Elvis Presley
12.  “I just know that there are a lot of people out there who are desperate for something different.” — Elvis Presley
13.  “I was training to be an electrician. I suppose I got wired the wrong way round somewhere along the line.” — Elvis Presley
14.  “Take care of the fans and they will sure as hell take care of you.” — Elvis Presley
15.  “The Lord can give, and the Lord can take away. I might be herding sheep next year.” — Elvis Presley
16.  “I don’t know anything about music. In my line you don’t have to.” — Elvis Presley
17.  “I get more joy out of singing than anything else, and I love it.” — Elvis Presley
18.  “Every time I think that I’m getting old, and gradually going to the grave, something else happens.” — Elvis Presley
19.  “I am just a simple country boy at heart.” — Elvis Presley
20.  “I’ve left my mark, and I hope it makes a difference.” — Elvis Presley
21.  “I always felt that someday, somehow, something would happen to change everything for me, and I’d daydream about how it would be.” — Elvis Presley
22.  “I ain’t no saint, but I’ve tried never to do anything that would hurt my family or offend God.” — Elvis Presley
23.  “I believe in the Bible. I believe that all good things come from God.” — Elvis Presley
24.  “I don’t do any vulgar movements.” — Elvis Presley
25.  “I’m so nervous. I’ve always been nervous, ever since I was a kid.” — Elvis Presley
26.  “People think you’re crazy if you talk about things they don’t understand.” — Elvis Presley
27.  “There is a season for everything, patience will reward you and reveal all answers to your questions.” — Elvis Presley
28.  “Music should be something that makes you gotta move, inside or outside.” — Elvis Presley
29.  “More than anything else, I want the folks back at home to think right of me.” — Elvis Presley
30.  “I’ll never feel comfortable taking a strong drink, and I’ll never feel easy smoking a cigarette. I just don’t think those things are right for me.” — Elvis Presley
31.  “I never said I was the King of Rock ‘n’ Roll. I said simply that I’m the best.” — Elvis Presley
32.  “When I first saw you with your smile so tender, my heart was captured, my soul surrendered.” — Elvis Presley
33.  “I just want to be treated like a regular customer.” — Elvis Presley
34.  “I’ll stay in Memphis.” — Elvis Presley
35.  “I’ve tried to lead a straight, clean life, not set any kind of a bad example.” — Elvis Presley
36.  “I am trying as hard as I can to give them the best that I can.” — Elvis Presley
37.  “All the stars will tell the story of our love and all its glory.” — Elvis Presley
38.  “I’m strictly for people.” — Elvis Presley
39.  “I was always different. I was always the weird one.” — Elvis Presley
40.  “I was a dreamer. I read comic books and I was the hero of the comic book.” — Elvis Presley
41.  “You have to be in the right place at the right time, but when it happens, you better be ready.” — Elvis Presley
42.  “I don’t know why people hate me. I’ve never hurt anyone.” — Elvis Presley
43.  “It’s always been my dream to come to Madison Square Garden and be the first person to sell it out two nights in a row.” — Elvis Presley
44.  “I knew by heart all the dialogue of James Dean’s films; I could watch Rebel Without a Cause a hundred times over.” — Elvis Presley
45.  I think most people have the illusion that a great many entertainers have a [happy life](/how-to-live-a-happy-life) and a great many do.” — Elvis Presley
46.  “I’m not interested in being a movie star. I’m interested in making good movies.” — Elvis Presley
47.  “From the time I was a kid, I always knew something was going to happen to me. Didn’t know exactly what.” — Elvis Presley
48.  “I don’t know anything about music. In my line you don’t have to.” — Elvis Presley
49.  “I’m no hillbilly singer.” — Elvis Presley
50.  “I’d like to be known as a good man who was dedicated to music and to his fans.” — Elvis Presley
