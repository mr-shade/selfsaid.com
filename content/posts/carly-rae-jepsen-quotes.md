“I think that being vulnerable is one of the most important things in life.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“Music is an outburst of the soul.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“It’s important to stay true to who you are and what makes you excited.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I really believe in the idea that you can have everything you want if you’re just willing to work for it.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I want to be honest in my music and lyrics, to be able to say, ‘This is what I’m feeling right now.'” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I like people who are a little bit wild at heart, a little bit rebellious.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“The idea of not being afraid to make mistakes, to take risks, is something that resonates with me.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I think it’s important to always keep challenging yourself, no matter what level you’re at.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I think love is the most magical thing that could happen to anyone.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“You can be strong and still be vulnerable at the same time.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“Sometimes the best way to inspire creativity is to just let go and see where it takes you.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
I’ve learned that there’s a beauty in [saying yes](https://selfsaid.30tools.com/say-yes-to-yourself) to the things that scare you.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“If you [stay positive](https://selfsaid.30tools.com/how-to-keep-a-positive-outlook-in-life) and optimistic, [good things can happen](https://selfsaid.30tools.com/how-to-make-good-things-happen).” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I’m always evolving as an artist, and that’s the beauty of music.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“Success for me is being able to make the music I love, and connecting with people through it.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I believe in the power of dreams and that you can achieve anything you set your mind to.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“The best part about music is that it can mean something different to everyone.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I think it’s important to stay true to your own voice, your own style, and your own heart.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“Creativity is about finding those [priceless moments](https://selfsaid.30tools.com/priceless-moments-money-cant-buy) of inspiration and following them, wherever they lead.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I’m drawn to people who are passionate about what they do.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“The most rewarding thing about music is the ability to connect with others on an emotional level.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I think vulnerability is what makes us human, and it’s something we should embrace.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I’m constantly seeking out new challenges to push myself creatively.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I think life is about finding the balance between chasing your dreams and enjoying the moment.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“To me, music is a form of therapy, a way to express the things we can’t always say out loud.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“There’s a strength in being soft and compassionate.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I think the best music comes from a place of truth.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“Every experience, good or bad, is a chance to learn something new about yourself.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“Being an artist means being open to the world and letting it inspire you.” – Carly Rae Jepsen  
  
“I want to create music that makes people feel something, that touches their hearts.” – Carly Rae Jepsen
