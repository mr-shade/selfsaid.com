Bob Dylan has shared countless thoughts on life, love, and music that continue to inspire and challenge people of all ages. These quotes capture his unique perspective and offer a glimpse into the mind of one of the most influential voices of our time.

## Bob Dylan Quotes

1.  “A hero is someone who understands the responsibility that comes with his freedom.” – Bob Dylan
2.  “I define nothing. Not beauty, not patriotism. I take each thing as it is, without prior rules about what it should be.” – Bob Dylan
3.  “Don’t criticize what you can’t understand.” – Bob Dylan
4.  “All I can do is be me, whoever that is.” – Bob Dylan
5.  “People seldom do what they believe in. They do what is convenient, then repent.” – Bob Dylan
6.  “I think of a hero as someone who understands the degree of responsibility that comes with his freedom.” – Bob Dylan
7.  “Sometimes it’s not enough to know what things mean, sometimes you have to know what things don’t mean.” – Bob Dylan
8.  “Act the way you’d like to be and soon you’ll be the way you act.” – Bob Dylan
9.  “There’s no success like failure, and failure’s no success at all.” – Bob Dylan
10.  “Democracy doesn’t rule the world, you better get that in your head; this world is ruled by violence, but I guess that’s better left unsaid.” – Bob Dylan
11.  “When you’ve got nothing, you’ve got nothing to lose.” – Bob Dylan
12.  “A mistake is to commit a misunderstanding.” – Bob Dylan
13.  “Being noticed can be a burden. Jesus got himself crucified because he got himself noticed. So I disappear a lot.” – Bob Dylan
14.  “All this talk about equality. The only thing people really have in common is that they are all going to die.” – Bob Dylan
15.  “You don’t need a weatherman to know which way the wind blows.” – Bob Dylan
16.  “I accept chaos, but I’m not sure whether it accepts me.” – Bob Dylan
17.  “People are crazy and times are strange.” – Bob Dylan
18.  “I gave myself to sin, and I’ve been forgiven.” – Bob Dylan
19.  “To live outside the law, you must be honest.” – Bob Dylan
20.  “People today are still living off the table scraps of the sixties. They are still being passed around – the music and the ideas.” – Bob Dylan
21.  “Money doesn’t talk, it swears.” – Bob Dylan
22.  “A song is anything that can walk by itself.” – Bob Dylan
23.  “I don’t break the rules because I don’t have any rules to break.” – Bob Dylan
24.  “I don’t believe you. You’re a liar!” – Bob Dylan
25.  “Chaos is a friend of mine.” – Bob Dylan
26.  “You better start swimming, or you’ll sink like a stone.” – Bob Dylan
27.  “Take care of all your memories. For you cannot relive them.” – Bob Dylan
28.  “The times they are a-changing.” – Bob Dylan
29.  “No one is free, even the birds are chained to the sky.” – Bob Dylan
30.  “You learn from a conglomeration of the incredible past – whatever experience gotten in any way whatsoever.” – Bob Dylan
31.  “A man is a success if he gets up in the morning and goes to bed at night and in between does what he wants to do.” – Bob Dylan
32.  “What’s money? A man is a success if he gets up in the morning and goes to bed at night and in between does what he wants to do.” – Bob Dylan
33.  “Behind every beautiful thing, there’s some kind of pain.” – Bob Dylan
34.  “All the truth in the world adds up to one big lie.” – Bob Dylan
35.  “Being on tour is like being in limbo. It’s like going from nowhere to nowhere.” – Bob Dylan
36.  “Life is more or less a lie, but then again, that’s exactly the way we want it to be.” – Bob Dylan
37.  “Inspiration is hard to come by. You have to take it where you find it.” – Bob Dylan
38.  “There’s no liquor in the land that can stop your brain from bleedin’.” – Bob Dylan
39.  “My father said, ‘You know, Bobby, when you meet someone, look them in the eye, don’t look down, look them in the eye.’ That stuck with me.” – Bob Dylan
40.  “No one is free until everyone is free.” – Bob Dylan
41.  “You can’t be wise and in love at the same time.” – Bob Dylan
42.  “I was born very far from where I’m supposed to be, and so I’m on my way home.” – Bob Dylan
43.  “Everything passes. Everything changes. Just do what you think you should do.” – Bob Dylan
44.  “The highest purpose of art is to inspire. What else can you do for anyone but inspire them?” – Bob Dylan
45.  “All the money you made will never buy back your soul.” – Bob Dylan
46.  “You can’t imagine parades of millions of people in different countries at the same time going through the streets demanding peace – and then nothing happens.” – Bob Dylan
47.  “Some people feel the rain. Others just get wet.” – Bob Dylan
48.  “It frightens me, the awful truth, of how sweet life can be.” – Bob Dylan
