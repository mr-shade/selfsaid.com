A collection of Gracie Abrams quotes on music, creativity, life, and love.

## Gracie Abrams Quotes

“Music has always been a form of therapy for me.” — Gracie Abrams

“I feel like I’m always trying to be as honest as I can be in everything I write.” — Gracie Abrams

“I love when music makes me feel understood, so I try to do that for other people too.” — Gracie Abrams

“Vulnerability in music is something that makes people feel less alone.” — Gracie Abrams

“Songwriting helps me process things I can’t even explain to myself.” — Gracie Abrams

“The internet has made it so easy to connect with people, and I love that aspect of it.” — Gracie Abrams

“I don’t think I’d be able to exist in the world if I couldn’t write music.” — Gracie Abrams

“I’m super shy, but when it comes to music, I feel like I can be my truest self.” — Gracie Abrams

“My goal is always to make music that feels true to me and hopefully resonates with others.” — Gracie Abrams

“I think the most special thing about music is that it brings people together, even if they’ve never met.” — Gracie Abrams

“I write my music mostly in my bedroom. It’s my safe space.” — Gracie Abrams

“I’ve always turned to music when I don’t know how else to cope with what I’m feeling.” — Gracie Abrams

“I’m still figuring things out, and music is the best way for me to do that.” — Gracie Abrams

“If I’m not honest in my songs, I feel like I’m doing a disservice to myself and anyone who listens.” — Gracie Abrams

“It’s really special when someone says my music has helped them in some way.” — Gracie Abrams

“Every song I write feels like a piece of me that I’m sharing with the world.” — Gracie Abrams

“Sometimes I don’t even realize how I’m feeling until I write a song about it.” — Gracie Abrams

“I want my music to feel like a conversation between me and the listener.” — Gracie Abrams

“The best songs come when you’re not overthinking it.” — Gracie Abrams

“Songwriting is like putting my thoughts in order when they feel scattered.” — Gracie Abrams

“I’ve always felt like music is the one thing that makes sense when nothing else does.” — Gracie Abrams

“I’ve learned so much from listening to other people’s stories through their music.” — Gracie Abrams

“Sometimes the simplest songs can carry the most weight.” — Gracie Abrams

“My fans feel like friends because they relate to the same emotions I write about.” — Gracie Abrams

“Songwriting is an extension of who I am; it’s how I make sense of the world.” — Gracie Abrams

“I think it’s really brave to be vulnerable, especially in the world we live in today.” — Gracie Abrams

“I never write for anyone else, but it’s such a gift when people connect with what I’m feeling.” — Gracie Abrams

“I always hope my music will find the people who need it at the right time.” — Gracie Abrams

“I’m grateful every day that I get to do what I love for a living.” — Gracie Abrams

“Music is the one place where I don’t feel the pressure to be perfect.” — Gracie Abrams

“There’s so much power in letting yourself feel deeply, even when it hurts.” — Gracie Abrams
