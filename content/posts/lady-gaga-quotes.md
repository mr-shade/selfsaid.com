Lady Gaga is known not only for her unique style and music, but also for her striking and inspirational quotes. Here you’ll find 50 of her best quotes that showcase her wisdom, humor, and indomitable spirit. Whether you’re a fan or just looking for a little inspiration, these quotes capture Lady Gaga’s message to the world.

1.  “Don’t you ever let a soul in the world tell you that you can’t be exactly who you are.” – Lady Gaga
2.  “I had a boyfriend who told me I’d never succeed, never be nominated for a Grammy, never have a hit song, and that he hoped I’d fail. I said to him, ‘Someday, when we’re not together, you won’t be able to order a cup of coffee at the fucking deli without hearing or seeing me.'” – Lady Gaga
3.  “You have to be unique, and different, and shine in your own way.” – Lady Gaga
4.  “Some women choose to follow men, and some women choose to follow their dreams. If you’re wondering which way to go, remember that your career will never wake up and tell you that it doesn’t love you anymore.” – Lady Gaga
5.  “I’m just trying to change the world, one sequin at a time.” – Lady Gaga
6.  “It’s always wrong to hate, but it’s never wrong to love.” – Lady Gaga
7.  “I think that promoting insecurity in the form of plastic surgery is infinitely more harmful than an artistic expression related to body modification.” – Lady Gaga
8.  “I’m not a feminist – I hail men, I love men. I celebrate American male culture, and beer, and bars and muscle cars…” – Lady Gaga
9.  “Do not allow people to dim your shine because they are blinded. Tell them to put on sunglasses.” – Lady Gaga
10.  “At the end of the day, you won’t be happy until you love yourself.” – Lady Gaga
11.  “You have to be your biggest fan. And when things are really tough and they’re really rough and nothing’s working – but there’s something inside of you that says, ‘I just have to follow that.’ Because you don’t know who you’re going to meet.” – Lady Gaga
12.  “I’ve been searching for ways to heal myself, and I’ve found that kindness is the best way.” – Lady Gaga
13.  “I really believe in the glamour of it all and that life is art and your imagination is the tool that you use to create and sculpt your existence.” – Lady Gaga
14.  “I am my own sanctuary and I can be reborn as many times as I choose throughout my life.” – Lady Gaga
15.  “What I’ve discovered is that in art, as in music, there’s a lot of truth—and then there’s a lie. The art is a lie that makes us realize truth.” – Lady Gaga
16.  “Ignore all hatred and criticism. Live for what you create, and die protecting it.” – Lady Gaga
17.  “I think it’s better to have a few great friends than a ton of fake ones.” – Lady Gaga
18.  “Why spend your whole life trying to be somebody that you’re not? It’s so much more fun to be yourself.” – Lady Gaga
19.  “Being a lady today means being a fighter. It means being a survivor.” – Lady Gaga
20.  “If you’re a strong female, you don’t need permission.” – Lady Gaga
21.  “To anyone that ever told you that you’re no good… They’re no better.” – Lady Gaga
22.  “[Confidence is key](/10-ways-to-appear-more-confident). If you don’t have it, fake it!” – Lady Gaga
23.  “Trust is like a mirror, you can fix it if it’s broken, but you can still see the crack in that mother fucker’s reflection.” – Lady Gaga
24.  “Fashion is that thing that saved me from being sad.” – Lady Gaga
25.  “A girl’s got to use what she’s given and I’m not going to make a guy drool the way a Britney video does. So I take it to extremes.” – Lady Gaga
26.  “I believe in a passion for inclusion.” – Lady Gaga
27.  “I don’t want to be remembered as anything but brave. The only good intention to make money is to help others.” – Lady Gaga
28.  “I don’t think I could think of a single thing that’s more isolating than being famous.” – Lady Gaga
29.  “I believe that if you have revolutionary potential, you must make the world a better place and use it.” – Lady Gaga
30.  “No matter who you are, or where you come from, or how much money you’ve got in your pocket, you have your own destiny and your own life ahead of you.” – Lady Gaga
31.  “If you’re a friend of somebody and they’re going through something, stick by them. They need you more than anything.” – Lady Gaga
32.  “People will always talk, so lets give them something to talk about.” – Lady Gaga
33.  “When you leave here tonight, know that at least one person believes in you.” – Lady Gaga
34.  “Pop music will never be low brow.” – Lady Gaga
35.  “I am not a piece of meat.” – Lady Gaga
36.  “Art is risk.” – Lady Gaga
37.  “I hope when I’m dead I’ll be considered an icon, though.” – Lady Gaga
38.  “I am my own work of art.” – Lady Gaga
39.  “Bare your struggles very close to your heart and allow yourself to feel them deeply.” – Lady Gaga
40.  “I believe fame is very unnatural. And I feel like if you don’t feed your creativity, your fame will go away.” – Lady Gaga
41.  “You laugh at me because I’m different, I laugh at you because you’re all the same.” – Lady Gaga
42.  “Prejudice is a disease. So is fashion. But I will not wear prejudice.” – Lady Gaga
43.  “I want women — and men — to feel empowered by a deeper and psychotic part of themselves. The part they’re always trying desperately to hide. I want that to become something that they cherish.” – Lady Gaga
44.  “Even if the whole world turns their back on you, you’ll always have yourself.” – Lady Gaga
45.  “Love is like a brick. You can build a house, or you can sink a dead body.” – Lady Gaga
46.  “I don’t want to be seen as a martyr or a dreamer but as someone who will do whatever it takes to make the world a better place.” – Lady Gaga
47.  “I don’t like Los Angeles. The people are awful and terribly shallow, and everybody wants to be famous but nobody wants to play the game.” – Lady Gaga
48.  “If you don’t have any shadows you’re not in the light.” – Lady Gaga
49.  “I feel that if you’re a truly artistic person, you have to fight.” – Lady Gaga
