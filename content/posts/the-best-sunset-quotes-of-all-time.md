What comes to mind when you watch a spectacular sunset? Maybe romantic feelings, a deep-felt love for life, or maybe a connection to the universe?

Everybody experiences a sunset differently. I can’t imagine anything more beautiful, awe-inspiring, or breathtaking than a sunset. I find it incredible that every day ends with a unique sunset, no two are ever the same. Think about that for a moment, out of all the sunsets over the last 13 billion years, they are all different!
