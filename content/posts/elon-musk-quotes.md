If you’re curious about what drives one of the most influential tech leaders of our time, you’ll enjoy reading this collection of Elon Musk’s 50 best quotes. From reflections on innovation to [thoughts on perseverance](/quotes-about-perseverance), these quotes offer a glimpse into the [successful mindset](/mindsets-for-success) of a man who continues to shape our future with every venture. 1.  “When something is important enough, you do it even if the odds are not in your favor.” – Elon Musk
2.  “Persistence is very important. You should not give up unless you are forced to give up.” – Elon Musk
3.  “I think it is possible for ordinary people to choose to be extraordinary.” – Elon Musk
4.  “Failure is an option here. If things are not failing, you are not innovating enough.” – Elon Musk
5.  “It’s OK to have your eggs in one basket as long as you control what happens to that basket.” – Elon Musk
6.  “Some people don’t like change, but you need to embrace change if the alternative is disaster.” – Elon Musk
7.  “The first step is to establish that something is possible; then probability will occur.” – Elon Musk
8.  “If you get up in the morning and think the future is going to be better, it is a bright day. Otherwise, it’s not.” – Elon Musk
9.  “Being an entrepreneur is like eating glass and staring into the abyss of death.” – Elon Musk
10.  “I could either watch it happen or be a part of it.” – Elon Musk
11.  “If something’s important enough you should try. Even if the probable outcome is failure.” – Elon Musk
12.  “I think most people can learn a lot more than they think they can. They sell themselves short without trying.” – Elon Musk
13.  “There are really two types of people. People who are compelled to try to do it. And people who are not.” – Elon Musk
14.  “I take the position that I’m always to some degree wrong, and the aspiration is to be less wrong.” – Elon Musk
15.  “People should pursue what they’re passionate about. That will make them happier than pretty much anything else.” – Elon Musk
16.  “It is possible for ordinary people to choose to be extraordinary.” – Elon Musk
17.  “You have to be pretty driven to make it happen. Otherwise, you will just make yourself miserable.” – Elon Musk
18.  “I think it’s very important to have a feedback loop, where you’re constantly thinking about what you’ve done and how you could be doing it better.” – Elon Musk
19.  “Life is too short for long-term grudges.” – Elon Musk
20.  “I don’t create companies for the sake of creating companies, but to get things done.” – Elon Musk
21.  “My motivation for all my companies has been to be involved in something that I thought would have a significant impact on the world.” – Elon Musk
22.  “I would like to die on Mars. Just not on impact.” – Elon Musk
23.  “I think it is important to have a good framework for thinking. It’s like a mental toolbox.” – Elon Musk
24.  “Patience is a virtue, and I’m learning patience. It’s a tough lesson.” – Elon Musk
25.  “Brand is just a perception, and perception will match reality over time.” – Elon Musk
26.  “If you’re trying to create a company, it’s like baking a cake. You have to have all the ingredients in the right proportion.” – Elon Musk
27.  “There’s a tremendous bias against taking risks. Everyone is trying to optimize their ass-covering.” – Elon Musk
28.  “Starting and growing a business is as much about the innovation, drive, and determination of the people behind it as it is about the product they sell.” – Elon Musk
29.  “The path to the CEO’s office should not be through the CFO’s office, and it should not be through the marketing department. It needs to be through engineering and design.” – Elon Musk
30.  “I think you should always bear in mind that entropy is not on your side.” – Elon Musk
31.  “Great companies are built on great products.” – Elon Musk
32.  “If you’re co-founder or CEO, you have to do all kinds of tasks you might not want to do… If you don’t do your chores, the company won’t succeed… No task is too menial.” – Elon Musk
33.  “I say something, and then it usually happens. Maybe not on schedule, but it usually happens.” – Elon Musk
34.  “I don’t think it’s a good idea to plan to sell a company.” – Elon Musk
35.  “Really pay attention to negative feedback and solicit it, particularly from friends. Hardly anyone does that, and it’s incredibly helpful.” – Elon Musk
36.  “We’re going to make it happen. As God is my bloody witness, I’m hell-bent on making it work.” – Elon Musk
37.  “I don’t spend my time pontificating about high-concept things; I spend my time solving engineering and manufacturing problems.” – Elon Musk
38.  “Land on Mars, a round-trip ticket – half a million dollars. It can be done.” – Elon Musk
39.  “If you need inspiring words, don’t do it.” – Elon Musk
40.  “Physics is a good framework for thinking. … Boil things down to their fundamental truths and reason up from there.” – Elon Musk
41.  “You get paid in direct proportion to the difficulty of problems you solve.” – Elon Musk
42.  “I think it’s very important to actively seek out and listen very carefully to negative feedback.” – Elon Musk
43.  “When Henry Ford made cheap, reliable cars, people said, ‘Nah, what’s wrong with a horse?’ That was a huge bet he made, and it worked.” – Elon Musk
44.  “I always invest my own money in the companies that I create. I don’t believe in the idea of being a pure manager who doesn’t own stakes in the company.” – Elon Musk
45.  “Optimism, pessimism, fuck that; we’re going to make it happen. As God is my bloody witness, I’m hell-bent on making it work.” – Elon Musk
46.  “When you struggle with a problem, that’s when you understand it.” – Elon Musk
47.  “Any product that needs a manual to work is broken.” – Elon Musk
48.  “What makes innovative thinking happen?… I think it’s really a mindset. You have to decide.” – Elon Musk
49.  “Your mind is like a parachute, it doesn’t work if it is not open.” – Elon Musk
50.  “I think there should be regulations on social media to the degree that it negatively affects the public good.” – Elon Musk
