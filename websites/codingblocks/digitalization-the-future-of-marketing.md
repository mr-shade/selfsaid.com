The growth of the web and the development of digital technology as a media group have had various significant and unforeseen impacts on 21st-century life. While everybody expected hi-tech innovations to make things quicker, no one anticipated the social effects such advanced media would have.

Digital Technology in 21st Century

With the previous and the ongoing augment of various small and large businesses in the market, competition is rising. Thus, **Digital Marketing has grown to become a more powerful tool than ever before**. In a scenario of intense competition, it has become necessary to have a good media presence. Thus, more and more businesses have started investing their resources in Digital Marketing, making it a hot buzz in the marketing structure.

So, there’s no doubt that **Digital Marketing can assume a ground-breaking role in the business world**. But what are the tools that marketers use to implement all marketing strategies for business growth hacking? And how exactly are these tools put to use by digital marketers today?

Have you ever received notifications about your favorite food from Zomato? Do you ever feel that you keep seeing ads for a particular product/service you searched for in the past? Is your Gmail account constantly flooded with emails from Amazon, Swiggy, and Myntra?

I am certain as someone living in a highly technological world, you have experienced these scenarios. Though have you ever wondered how all of this is happening? Yes, you guessed it right; it all happens through the use of Digital Technology, data in particular.

**Data is king in the digital world**, and a good digital marketer can rule the kingdom. With the growing Digital Transformations, marketing experts now have more control over specialized data metrics. All this transformation helps to understand and control one’s Target Audience and the Digital Funnel. Thus, it becomes clear that **Digital Marketing is the future and the only key is to understand and learn it**.
