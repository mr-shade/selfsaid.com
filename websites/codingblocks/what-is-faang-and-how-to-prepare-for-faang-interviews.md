Employment at Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, Google (FAANG) is a fantasy fulfilled for almost all engineering students/graduates. Every year, FAANG tops the rundown list of the most sought-after companies for students coming from an engineering background. So, what is so special about FAANG that each engineering graduate aims to get into one of these companies?

_Following are some of the reasons why the_ [**_FAANG companies_**](https://online.codingblocks.com/courses/interview-questions-for-big-companies?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=course&utm_campaign=cards) _receive over 50k interview applications per week and continue being the most in-demand employers for engineers:_

**· Working Environment** – The FAANG organizations are considered to provide the perfect working environment to all employees. The FAANG working environment is fluid which provides space for comfort, creativity, learning, productivity, interaction, exposure, etc.

![](https://blog.codingblocks.com/content/images/2020/06/google-workplace.jpg)

Work-Space

**· Packages** – The FAANG organizations offer insane salary packages to their employers. Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, Google have set the bar for tech-geeks being paid immensely all over the world today. The top giants are said to pay bonuses as high as $100K to their employees. The incredibly marvelous monetary offerings make FAANG the next wall street success in the tech world.

![](https://blog.codingblocks.com/content/images/2020/06/packages.png)

Salary Packages

**· Additional Perks** – FAANG organizations are known to provide the best additional benefits for the employees. Some of the benefits include health benefits, food, transportation inclusion, paid leaves, stipend for training, parental leaves, etc. The perks act as a cherry on the top of the manifold advantages of working in Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, and Google.

![](https://blog.codingblocks.com/content/images/2020/06/employee-benefits.jpg)

Additional Employee Benefits

**· Repute** – Having worked in any of the FAANG organizations add not only to your exposure, lifestyle, and skills, but also immensely benefit your prospects. Having a FAANG on your resume makes you stand out from the large competition in the tech world.

![](https://blog.codingblocks.com/content/images/2020/06/faang--a-.png)
